About Me

Hi, I'm Ven Perov

G'day! I'm an Adelaide-based photographer, and I've been capturing moments professionally for about ten years now. Like a lot of people, I first picked up a proper camera back in my teens and pretty quickly got hooked on trying to capture the world around me – the landscapes, the city, the people. It evolved from there into a genuine passion and eventually, my career.

My approach is pretty relaxed and natural. I focus on capturing genuine moments and authentic expressions, rather than anything too staged. Think clean, vibrant images that really show off the subject or the atmosphere. Whether it's a wedding, family photos, professional headshots, or showcasing a property or product, the aim is always the same: high-quality, memorable photos that tell the right story.

Based in Adelaide, I love getting out and making the most of what SA has to offer. When I'm not behind the lens, you'll likely find me pushing my limits somewhere – whether that's training for a marathon, competing in judo, hiking rugged trails, or seeking out the next thrill like cliff diving, skydiving or hitting the road on my motorcycle. That sense of adventure and focus definitely carries over into my photography work. But balancing that out, getting to know my clients and understanding exactly what you're looking for is key to making sure we create photos you'll be stoked with.

Explore My Services Get In Touch

My Approach

Good photos come from good collaboration. I work closely with clients to understand their vision and goals. During a shoot, I aim for a comfortable, easy-going vibe – no awkward posing, just natural interactions and genuine moments captured well.

I combine technical know-how with a creative eye to deliver polished, professional images you'll love. From the first enquiry to the final photos, you can expect a straightforward process and clear communication all the way.
